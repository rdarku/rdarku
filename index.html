About Me

Hi, I'm Robert. I know code.

I have always wanted to say that.

I got introduced to coding back in high school when a friend showed me a web page he web page he had built in notepad. My mind was blown. If he could do it, so could I ( .... is what I told myself). What started as a dare from my friend turned me on this holy path of coding. I am now a life-long learner and a polyglot programmer, and I'm not stopping anytime soon.

I have had the pleasant opportunity to work with multiple teams of talented individuals, both technical and non-technical. I have had the honor of leading some of these teams. I love collaborative work and I'm always excited by the new challenges that pop up and intrigued about the smart solutions too. I have also had experience in customer service and technical support on two continents.

I am looking for new opportunities to leverage my expertise and experience in furtherance of a good cause.
So contact me. Let's get stuff done.
